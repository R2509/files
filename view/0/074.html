Access deviations with RSS feeds

Most of DeviantArt's searching and browsing capabilities are exposed through RSS. Specifically, we support Media RSS.

Here is an example query, searching for the term frogs, ordered by all-time popularity, in the digital art/drawings category:

https://backend.deviantart.com/rss.xml?type=deviation&q=boost%3Apopular+in%3Adigitalart%2Fdrawings+frogs

And here's the feed of newest deviations by artist spyed:

https://backend.deviantart.com/rss.xml?type=deviation&q=by%3Aspyed+sort%3Atime+meta%3Aall
